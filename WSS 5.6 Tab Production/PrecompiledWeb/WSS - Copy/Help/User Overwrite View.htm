User Overwrite View

 

Purpose:

 

This function will give you a very flexible choice when you are selecting the display of a screen or the selection in a batch report.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Column in picture named A/D stands for Ascending and Descending which will state in what order the records in that column will be displayed.

 

SO stands for Sorting. Than is how the priority off the sorting will be. Unless anything is stated it is gone be in the order they are selected. 1 in the field will make that specific column higher ranked in the sorting. The 2 will follow etc. Then it sorts the once without numbers as they are set in the selection.

 

FA stands for Factor. This is for limiting the selection of records belonging to that field. A Factor could be such as Equal (=), Less Than (<), More Than (>) etc… When you choose a factor you also have to choose a value
