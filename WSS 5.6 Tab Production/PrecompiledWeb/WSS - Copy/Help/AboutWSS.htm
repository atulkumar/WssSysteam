About WSS

 

WSS (Web support system) is an Online call/request tracking system. It’s a a multi-purpose

Comprehensive Online tool for providing online support to global clients of a company.

 

WSS provides detailed follow-up of Tasks and their actions against each call. Clients can track on urgent

call/task either through email or SMS. It is the best online solution to manage your

projects anywhere, anytime and from any browser.

 

This system aims for the enhanced productivity and complete management of the company resources and

acts as a reservoir for all kinds of Company's documents and records at one single

location. It focuses on the completion of projects, not just on the

planning of projects.

 

Provides facility for generating Invoices and Bills on the basis of the transactions stored in the WSS database.

Dynamic Reports generation for complete analysis of the records.

 

Intimation of new call/task entry in WSS via auto generated emails and SMS It improves an organization’s

productivity. Get projects done more quickly and intelligently. Plans all stages of

software development, implement construction and engineering projects.

 

WSS helps our customers to monitor their system offsite as per contracts. It can monitor things like your

hard disc space your server’s status etc.

 

WSS helps user to create predefine template for regular calls/tasks. It’s also useful for creating Change

Management forms according to the client’s need.

 

WSS provides security based on company and Role and easy to copy one role

Overall WSS is about getting things done.
