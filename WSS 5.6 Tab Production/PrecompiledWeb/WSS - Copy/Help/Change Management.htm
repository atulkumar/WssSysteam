The Form Head Screen displays the list of all the forms. On double clicking the Form, it opens the Form

Entry screen. We can edit the form details and add more details and save the changes.

 

 

To create a New Form:

 

To create a New Form, click on the New Button , it will open the FORM ENTRY SCREEN

To Edit a Form:

 

Double Click the Form, that you want to Edit , it will open the Form Details Screen, make necessary changes

to the Form, and after Editing the Form, click on the OK button . It will save the changes made to the

existing Form.

To Delete a Record, click on the DELETE button .

And lastly, to cancel all the changes, click on the CANCEL button . All the information, that was not saved

will be lost.
